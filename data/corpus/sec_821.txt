The provisions of this chapter conferring jurisdiction upon courts-martial do not deprive
military
commissions, provost courts, or other
military
tribunals of concurrent jurisdiction with respect to offenders or offenses that by statute or by the law of war may be tried by
military
commissions, provost courts, or other
military
tribunals. This section does not apply to a
military
commission established under
chapter 47A of this title
.
