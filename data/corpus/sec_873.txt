At any time within three years after the date of the entry of judgment under
section 860c of this title
(article 60c), the accused may petition the
Judge Advocate General
for a new trial on the grounds of newly discovered evidence or fraud on the court. If the accused’s case is pending before a Court of Criminal Appeals or before the Court of Appeals for the Armed Forces, the
Judge Advocate General
shall refer the petition to the appropriate court for action. Otherwise the
Judge Advocate General
shall act upon the petition.
