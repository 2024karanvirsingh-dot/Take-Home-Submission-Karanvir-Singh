Any person subject to this chapter—
(1)
who, having been a prisoner as the result of a court-martial conviction or other criminal proceeding, is on parole with conditions; and
(2)
who violates the conditions of parole;
shall be punished as a court-martial may direct.

Pub. L. 114–328, div. E, title LX, § 5420
,

,
