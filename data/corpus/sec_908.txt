Any person subject to this chapter who, without proper authority—
(1)
sells or otherwise disposes of;
(2)
willfully or through neglect damages, destroys, or loses; or
(3)
willfully or through neglect suffers to be lost, damaged, destroyed, sold, or wrongfully disposed of;
any
military
property of the United States, shall be punished as a court-martial may direct.
(Aug. 10, 1956, ch. 1041,
70A Stat. 71
.)
