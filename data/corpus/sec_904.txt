Any person subject to this chapter who, willfully and unlawfully—
(1)
alters, conceals, removes, mutilates, obliterates, or destroys a public
record
; or
(2)
takes a public
record
with the intent to alter, conceal, remove, mutilate, obliterate, or destroy the public
record
;
shall be punished as a court-martial may direct.
(Added
Pub. L. 114–328, div. E, title LX, § 5415
,
Dec. 23, 2016
,
130 Stat. 2944
.)
